This Castlevania BGM resource pack silences Minecraft's default music and replaces it with tracks from Castlevania Dracula X and Symphony of the Night.

<br>

## Features

#### Extensive Music Coverage
Maps many tracks to various biomes, title and death screens, dimensions, boss encounters, and enemy encounters.

<br>

## Good to Know

#### Requires Maestro
Maestro: Overture is a music resource pack and requires the
[Maestro](https://modrinth.com/mod/maestro-music) mod to function. Without Maestro, the pack will not affect music playback beyond silencing Minecraft's music tracks.

I also recommend disabling the Minecraft music in the Maestro mod config.
